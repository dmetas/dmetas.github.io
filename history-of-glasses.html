Glasses:

Glasses have been around since the year 1286. The oldest record of them being found in Italy. There are those who believe that China had invented glasses at an earlier date but there is no documented proof of this.

Historically why people wore glasses:

  1. To improve vision
  2. Light sensitivity
  3. Fashion
